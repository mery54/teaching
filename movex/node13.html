2.4 Assessment

The assessment of students is based on three works:

  • Two written exams: E1 and E2
  • A practical exam: TP.
  • E1 is shared by MALG1/MOVEX1.
  • E2 is specific to MALG2 and MOVEX2.
  • TP is specific to MALG2 and MOVEX2.
