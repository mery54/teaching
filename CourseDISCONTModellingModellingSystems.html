6 Course DISCONT Modelling Modelling Systems

The course Modelling Modelling Systems (DISCONT) is taught in the Master in Computer Science of the University of Lorraine and in the Master in Computer Engineering of Telecom Nancy. The lectures notes and documents for the students are at the link DISCONT. The lectures have been designed in the ANR project DISCONT and it has been taught two times for assessing it.
